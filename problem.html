October 7th

What is it?

October 7th is when Hamas (a palestinian based terrorist organization) attacked Israel. They launched at least “2,500 rockets,“ killed “1,163 people,” and took “240 civilians and soldiers” as hostages (Aljazeera, Agence France Presse, Saidel et al.).
