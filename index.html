The use of Force Against Hamas

Context

After the collapse of the Ottoman empire, people like Theodore Herzl wanted to create a Jewish state where their ancestors once lived. This is called Zionism. After World War One, Zionists convinced the Brittish and League of nations to place a mandate over the area of Palestine through self determination. However, the mandate brought with it some problems. The local arab population opposed the mandate and Palestinians revolted in the Arab Revolt of 1936-39. The British government shipped thousands of troops while the local Jewish population created a militia called the Haganah (although not allowed by the British).
